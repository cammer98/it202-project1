About

  • 1987

    Graduation

    Ken graduated from Augustana College with a Bachelors in Accounting.

  • 1988

    IDES

    Ken received his first job after college as an auditor/accountant for the Illinois Department of Employment Security where he met his wife Rose.

  • 1989

    An Agency Is Born

    In January of 1989, Ken started his business "Kenneth Harris CPA".

  • 1997

    Career

    Ken was hired as an accountant supervisor for the Illinois Department of Corrections. He was later promoted to business Administrator and worked their until his retirement.

  • 2018

    Retirement

    In September, Ken finished his long career as an accountant/business administrator for Stateville Correction Center
